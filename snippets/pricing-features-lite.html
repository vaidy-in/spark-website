Spark Lite features

Live teaching

  • Group classes, workshops, and personal 1:1 sessions
  • Zoom and Google Meet integration
  • Scheduling with conflict checks
  • Guest trainers and trial access for prospects
  • Video recordings and video bundles

Student portal and website

  • One-page marketing website for your studio
  • Single portal for schedules, subscriptions, and replays
  • Timezone-aware schedules and one-click join
  • Google sign-in for students

Payments and plans

  • Stripe (international) and Razorpay (India)
  • UPI, cards, netbanking, and wallets in India
  • Country-wise pricing and geo-pricing (bundled)
  • Subscriptions, one-time payments, donation, private, and tiered plans
  • Segments, discount codes, payment links, and payment reminders

Operations

  • Unlimited students; one active student across multiple Lite services counts once
  • Team roles and attendance tracking
  • Google Drive integration (bundled)
