Spark features

Course content

  • Chapters and modules from your video
  • Verified study notes per chapter
  • Slides, math and LaTeX
  • Dead air removal and HLS streaming
  • Video processing with credits (trial includes 5 free credits)

Learning experience

  • 3-pane player (video, notes, slides)
  • Semantic search across content
  • AI tutor grounded in your content
  • Quizzes (learning and evaluation mode)
  • Live quiz report (per student, per question)

Payments and commerce

  • Stripe (international) and Razorpay (India)
  • UPI, netbanking, wallets, credit and debit cards in India
  • Country-wise pricing and geo-pricing (bundled)
  • 90+ currencies for international students
  • Paid, free, donation, private, and tiered plans
  • Segments, discount codes, payment links, and reminders

Live operations (via Spark Lite)

  • Group classes, workshops, and 1:1 (Zoom and Meet)
  • Scheduling, conflict checks, guest trainers
  • Student portal for courses, live classes, and replays
  • One-page marketing website for your studio

Students and team

  • Unlimited students with segment-based visibility
  • Team roles: Senior Manager, Manager, Trainer, Student
  • Google Drive integration (bundled)
