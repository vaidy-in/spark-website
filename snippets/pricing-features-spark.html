Spark features

Course content

  • Live cohort programs and self-paced courses
  • Free teaser sessions for prospects
  • Chapters and modules from your recordings
  • Verified study notes per chapter
  • Slides and diagrams carried into study notes
  • Trim long pauses from chapter videos; smooth playback on slow connections
  • Turn recordings into course video (processing credits; trial includes 5)

Learning experience

  • One screen: video, notes, and slides together
  • Search across your course to jump to a topic or phrase
  • AI tutor grounded in your content
  • Practice quizzes and graded quizzes
  • Live quiz report (per student, per question)

Payments and commerce

  • Stripe (international) and Razorpay (India)
  • UPI, netbanking, wallets, credit and debit cards in India
  • Country-wise pricing and geo-pricing (bundled)
  • 90+ currencies for international students
  • Segments and discount codes for course launches and learner groups

Students and team

  • Segment learners for targeted offers, messages, and course access
  • Admin, manager, and trainer access for assistants and co-teachers
  • Google Drive integration (bundled)
